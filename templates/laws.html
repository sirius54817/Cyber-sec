Essential Cyber Laws

Understanding the legal framework that protects your digital rights

Computer Fraud and Abuse Act (CFAA)

A comprehensive law that addresses computer-related fraud and unauthorized access to protected computers and networks. It provides legal protection against hacking, malware, and other cyber threats.

  • Protects against unauthorized access
  • Covers computer fraud
  • Federal level protection

General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

European Union law on data protection and privacy, affecting organizations worldwide. Sets standards for data collection, processing, and storage.

  • Data privacy protection
  • User consent requirements
  • Global impact

Electronic Communications Privacy Act (ECPA)

Protects wire, oral, and electronic communications while in transit. Addresses privacy concerns in the digital age.

  • Communication privacy
  • Data transmission security
  • Digital rights protection

Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)

Protects the privacy of children under 13 years of age online. Sets standards for data collection and usage.

  • Child privacy protection
  • Parental consent requirements
  • Online safety measures
